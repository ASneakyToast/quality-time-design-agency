## The Program

Our workshop series brings together designers, students, and curious makers for hands-on explorations of design craft. Each session focuses on a specific technique or concept, from risograph printing to brand strategy.

## Format

- Half-day intensive sessions
- Maximum 12 participants for personalized attention
- All materials provided
- Take-home resources and templates

## Topics Covered

We've hosted workshops on risograph printing, brand identity systems, editorial design, and collaborative creative processes. Each workshop is designed to leave participants with both practical skills and a finished piece they're proud of.
