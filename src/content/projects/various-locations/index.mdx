## The Project

Various Locations is a platform for curated selections of premium artistic venues across Beijing, Shanghai, and Shenzhen — bridging culture gaps between the US and China.

## Identity

The identity employs bullet points as geographic location markers, blending map symbolism with GPS navigation coordinates. The system translates the concept of place and coordinates into a clean, contemporary visual language.

## Web Design

Alongside the identity, we designed and built the platform website at variouslocationsnewyork.com, creating a digital home for their curated venue listings and editorial content.
