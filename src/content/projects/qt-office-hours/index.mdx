## The Exhibition

Quality Time Office Hours was a publication exhibition at CCA's MFA Design Studio, running November 2-16, 2025. The space merged professional output and personal practice in a hybrid environment combining office, living room, and library functions.

## Concept

Visitors encountered various projects and catalogues designed for viewing, reflection, and copyingâ€”positioning these works simultaneously as art and reference materials. The studio operated as both gallery and workspace, deliberately infiltrating the 9-to-5 and blurring the lines between cultural input and output.

## Local Archive

The exhibition showcased selected Bay Area galleries and presses, bringing a shared local archive into CCA's MFA studio. This emphasized how local creative communities form the foundational infrastructure supporting institutional spaces.
