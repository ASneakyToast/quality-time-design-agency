## The Event

Bug Fair Connected was a virtual event hosted by the Natural History Museum of LA County, bringing the beloved annual insect celebration to digital audiences.

## Visual Approach

The visual expression was approached with a pop-art direction, creating bold and vibrant graphics that celebrate the world of insects.

## Deliverables

Production includes print and digital assets utilized for on-site signage and livestream backdrops, featuring custom insect illustrations and animated graphics.
