## The Exhibition

Foul Odor: An Exhibition on Survival was a group show presented at Upper Market Gallery in San Francisco, curated by Off Hours.

## Design Approach

Working alongside Savithri Velaga, we designed a digital-hybrid exhibition identity that aligned with the DIY essence of the show. The visual system bridges physical and digital presentation, maintaining the grassroots character of the exhibition while creating a cohesive identity across platforms.
