## The Client

Angelika Film Center is the most successful and recognized arthouse cinema in the United States. Operating since 1989, the flagship SoHo Cinema location specializes in debuting and promoting groundbreaking independent and international films.

## The Project

A 7-week collaboration developing a comprehensive pitch and storytelling proposal from inception through strategy, editorial design, and visual execution. The etymological foundation—Angelika derived from Greek meaning "messenger"—informed the design direction.

## Deliverables

Visual design materials, branding strategy, and pitch presentation emphasizing the organization's historical significance, distinctive tone, and brand identity.
