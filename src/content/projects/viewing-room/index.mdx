## The Exhibition

Viewing Room is a salon-style photo book exhibition showcasing selected works from small presses around the world. The exhibition served as a companion event to the LA Art Book Fair, taking place at Meier Stâ€”a new arts space and residency in Los Angeles.

## Experience

The format invites visitors to peruse and meditate on selected books in an intimate setting, encouraging slow engagement with the publications on display.

## Participating Presses

The exhibition featured 18 independent publishing entities including Book Dummy Press, Delvaz Projects, Dancing Foxes, For the Birds Trapped in Airports, Cash Machine LA, Zatara Press, Charcoal Book Club, From Makan, Tall Poppy Press, Miau Ediciones, Coloured Publishing, Atelier Editions, Blum Gallery, and others.

## Collaborators

Organized by Maria Cardenas and Savithri Velaga in collaboration with Takashi Yanai at Meier St. Graphics by Andrew Roque.
