## The Venue

Hollywood Forever Cemetery has hosted outdoor film screenings for over two decades, drawing thousands of Angelenos to watch classic films under the stars among historic monuments.

## The Brief

Create a cohesive visual identity for the summer season that honors the venue's unique atmosphere while feeling fresh and contemporary.

## Deliverables

- Season poster series
- Event programs
- Environmental wayfinding
- Social media templates
- Merchandise including blankets and tote bags
