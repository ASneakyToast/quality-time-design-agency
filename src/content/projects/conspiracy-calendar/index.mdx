## The Idea

What if we took the absurdist humor of conspiracy theories and gave them the serious design treatment they absolutely don't deserve? The Conspiracy Calendar was born from late-night conversations and a shared appreciation for the weird corners of the internet.

The calendar became an unexpected viral hit, with features in Dezeen and It's Nice That celebrating its deadpan approach to absurdist content.
