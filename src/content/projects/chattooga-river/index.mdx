## The Anniversary

Chattooga Conservancy commemorates the 50th anniversary of the Wild and Scenic designation for the Chattooga River through a collection of commemorative ephemera.

## Deliverables

The project includes branded merchandise—shirts, stickers, and postcards—created to celebrate this significant environmental milestone for the river's protected status.
